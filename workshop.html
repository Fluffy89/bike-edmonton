WORKSHOP

Edmonton operates a fully-equipped, volunteer-run, community bike workshop.

New At This?

New At This?

Don't know anything about bicycle repairs?

No problem!

Volunteer mechanics are on duty to assist you, from finding a bike that's right for you, adjusting your seat, or fixing a flat, right up to building an entire bike from just parts.

We Will...

We Will...

  • You can learn how to fix your bike at either location
  • You can donate used bikes or parts to the workshop
  • You can buy refurbished used bicycles from the workshop
  • You can buy used bike parts and a limited selection of new parts as well
  • We operate the oldest community bike shop program in North America
  • You don’t need to know anything about bike repair: our volunteer mechanics will teach you

We Wont...

We Wont...

  • We do not allow DIY bleeding of hydraulics in our shops
  • Our ability to service some modern or higher-end components and suspension, is limited, if you're uncertain, please contact us to confirm
  • We do not allow gas powered bikes in our shops
  • We generally will not check stock of bikes & parts over the phone or email, please visit our shops during our regular hours to see what is available
  • You cannot store your bike at our shop and we cannot place items on hold

Our vision is a city where cycling is safe, widespread, and accessible to people of all comfort levels.
